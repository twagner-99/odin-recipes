Minestrone Soup

Description

Minestrone soup is a comforting Italian classic. It's full of healthy vegetables and is quite simple to make so it's great for a quick weeknight dinner.

Continue scrolling to see how to make it yourself!

Ingredients List

  • Homemade or storebought chicken stock
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can canellini or great nothern beans
  • 1 can red kidney beans
  • 1 chopped yellow onion
  • A bunch of other stuff

Steps

  1. Make your mirepoix
  2. Sautee your mirepoix
  3. Add garlic and tomatoes
  4. Add chicken stock
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Bring to a simmer
  7. Add everything else
