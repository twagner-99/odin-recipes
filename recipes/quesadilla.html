Quesadilla

Description

Who doesn't love a simple cheese quesadilla? Easy to make, cheap, and delicious, it's no wonder this Mexican staple is a favorite.

Continue reading for a foolproof recipe!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (flour or corn)
  • Shredded colby jack cheese

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Set down tortilla
  3. Add cheese
  4. Fold and flip!
